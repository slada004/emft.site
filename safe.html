SAFETY OF FUNDS
Leader in the Industry
Emfxtrade is the award-winning Global Leader in Online Trading. With over 200 instruments in forex, spot metals and CFDs on US and UK stocks, spot indices and commodities. Emfxtrade serves retail and institutional customers from over 180 countries in Europe, Asia, the Middle East, Africa and Latin America.

Accounts with Top Tier Banks
Emfxtrade provides banking and treasury services through world-class banking institutions to its clients. These banks are completely independent and do not endorse the obligations of Emfxtrade to its customers.

Segregation of Client Funds
Client funds security is a key element of the Emfxtrade philosophy alongside unmatched trading conditions and customer support. With global recognition for excellence, solid reputation and multi-award winning services, our clients are assured that their funds are held with security, clarity and integrity. Emfxtrade maintains segregated client accounts to ensure that client funds are fully separated from Emfxtrade' own funds at all times. Each client account has a unique code to ensure that it is used solely for client funding purposes. All client funds deposited are completely segregated in accordance with strict policies and procedures. Clients can be assured that their funds are safe.

Risk Management
Emfxtrade through its award-winning trading platforms provides an automated risk-management system to ensure that client account balances will never go negative.
